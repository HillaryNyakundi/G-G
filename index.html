The story behind GUARANTOR'S GUARD

Sacco Members get access to loans through guarantors consent on application. In the unfortunate events if the borrower fails to pay and the loan is not performing, the guarantors carry liability that is not in their budget. Either loosing their sacco deposit or failing their financial goal. G&G's goal is to make sure guarantors don't suffer liability to a loan they have not borrowed.
